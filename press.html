About BoomEpic Media

BoomEpic Media is a next-generation creator network built on 22 years of gaming expertise. Founded by a veteran creator turned agency owner, the network bridges the gap between authentic gaming culture and Fortune 500-grade media execution. Properties include BoomEpicKill (Entertainment), Boom Epic Live (Strategy), and DropRate.gg (Direct-to-Consumer).
